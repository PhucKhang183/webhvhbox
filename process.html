    2.

    COMPARATIVE QUOTING

    REFINED PRICING & STRATEGIC OPTIONS

    To help clients make informed decisions, we offer detailed quoting options based on different production quantities, materials, finishes, and design solutions. By breaking down specific production costs, we demonstrate how each adjustment impacts overall pricing-allowing for smart trade-offs between budget, function, and brand experience.

    COMPARATIVE QUOTING

    To provide the basis for an informed decision to our clients, parallel to the creative support process we offer variants of product features and design solutions. We quote price options (exact or in a range) for production of different quantities and sizes with choices for materials and finishes, breaking down specific production costs to demonstrate the impact of each change.

    3.

    Step 3: Pre-Production Verification

    ENSURING ACCURACY BEFORE FULL PRODUCTION

    We offer pre-production samples as an optional service to validate every detail before moving to mass production. These samples are created using the same equipment and production line, ensuring realistic and reliable results. Depending on the project, unprinted or printed samples-or both-may be provided as part of the quality verification stages.

    For certain projects, the final unit cost is confirmed upon completion of this stage.

    4.

    Step 4: Manufacturing

    Tailored Production, Craftsmanship Included

    Once the order is confirmed, our in-house production process begins. Every stage of crafting your custom luxury packaging is carried out under one roof for optimal quality control and efficiency.

    We embrace innovation in manufacturing, while honoring traditional craftsmanship and the value of skilled human hands in every fold and finish.

    5.

    Step 5: Delivery Management

    GLOBAL SHIPPING, FLEXIBLE LOGISTICS

    Once your premium packaging is produced and packed, we coordinate global shipping with care and precision. Whether handled by HVHBox or your logistics partner, delivery is available worldwide.

    Choose from express or economy shipping via DHL, or opt for bulk freight forwarding for larger volumes. Our delivery solutions are designed to ensure your packaging arrives safely and on schedule.
