Paper Box by Shapes Drawer Box

Elegant packaging with a sliding surprise

The drawer box is a refined packaging solution designed to elevate the unboxing experience. With its smooth sliding mechanism, the inner tray gracefully emerges from the outer shell, resembling the opening of a personal drawer.

This structure offers both visual appeal and functional protection, making it ideal for luxury items such as cosmetics, jewelry, electronics, and gift sets. The rigid outer sleeve ensures durability, while the drawer can be customized with ribbons, thumb notches, or magnetic catches for added usability.

Drawer boxes are highly versatile in format and finishing, allowing for full creative expression while maintaining structural integrity.

    BOX ANATOMY

    Structural Elements of the Drawer Box

    The drawer box is crafted with layered precision to ensure both elegance and functionality. Its core structure includes:

    OUTER SLEEVE

    A rigid, enclosed shell that houses the inner tray. Typically made from 2mm greyboard and wrapped in premium paper, it forms the protective outer frame.

    INNER TRAYS (DRAWER)

    A pull-out compartment designed to slide smoothly in and out. It can be fitted with custom inserts (foam, card, or molded) to secure products inside.

    PULL MECHANISM

    Options include ribbon pulls, thumb notches, or die-cut handles - enhancing both convenience and aesthetic appeal.

    STOPPER (OPTIONAL)

    A built-in stop-lock mechanism or paperboard edge can be added to prevent the drawer from fully detaching.

    BINDING MATERIALS

    Both sleeve and tray are wrapped in luxury papers or textiles, with options for contrast or tone-on-tone interior/exterior finishes.

    This structure is ideal for high-end unboxing moments and can be fully tailored to your product type, branding, and user experience goals.
